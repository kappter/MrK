Service Projects

Information

The Skyline TSA Chapter is very service-oriented, having participated in many service projects.

This past year, the chapter has ran a service project for the organization KaBOOM's Map of Play. KaBOOM's Map of Play is an organization that tries to make a comprehensive map of playgrounds for the use of parents seeking a safe, enjoyable playground for their kids. However, due to the scope of the service project, they rely on people in the community reviewing their local playground, and then uploading their reviews to the site.

In addition, the Skyline TSA Chapter is planning a visit to the local Food Bank. There, they would assist the Food Bank in sorting the donations they receive into the proper containers. This allows for a streamlined and efficient process in getting the food to those who need it.
