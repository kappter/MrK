2015 Webmaster Design Brief































Overview

E-Waste: Renewable Solutions for a Disposable Society

Information

Every year, TSA hosts the Webmaster Competition. One criteria is to have (a) page(s) on that year's Design Brief, a topic that the team must explain in an interesting and intuitive way. This year's Design Brief is to educate our community on the hazards of and solutions to E-Waste.
