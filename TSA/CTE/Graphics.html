Graphics

Information

This course gives students education and experince with applications such as Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photobooth, as well as a few others. Students get hands-on experience with these applications for use in a variety of real-world applications, such as stickers, magnets, notepads and t-shirts. With the skills they build in this course, students gain an edge for post-secondary education and their career. Appealing to all students, regardless of current skill level
