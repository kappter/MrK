Hello and welcome. I am a technology teacher at Skyline High in Salt Lake City, Utah. My students learn web development, programming, design and nearly every aspect of digital media. I believe in simple elegance.

A note about the site and some philosophy... a constant negotiation between logic and feeling. A balance between thought and emotion. Most of us actually point to our head when we analyze and lay a flattened hand to our chest when moved by emotion. It can often be a struggle to make peace with these forces, yet it can be a source of entertainment when you are aware.
